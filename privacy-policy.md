# Privacy Policy

**App Name:** ZinteBar

**Developer:** Sahil Ahmad Najar

**Last Updated:** June 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

ZinteBar ("we", "our", "us") respects the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it when you use the ZinteBar app or website.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Full name
- Email address
- Phone number (10-digit mobile number)
- Password (encrypted by Firebase Authentication — we never store it directly)
- Full delivery/home address
- District selected by user

### Order & Booking Information
- Service booked (food, cab, medical, stay)
- Order details, items, instructions
- Pickup/drop locations (for cab bookings)
- Appointment dates and times
- Order status history and timestamps

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

- To create and manage your ZinteBar account
- To connect you with local service providers
- To process and track your bookings and orders
- To send push notifications about order status updates
- To allow service providers to contact you regarding your booking
- To improve app performance, features, and services
- To maintain order history linked to your account

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data to any third party. Your information is shared only in these cases:

- **Service Providers** — your name, phone number, address, and order details are shared with the relevant service provider to fulfil your booking
- **Firebase (Google)** — for authentication, database storage, and push notifications
- **Legal Requirements** — if required by law or government authority

---

## 5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

- **Firebase Authentication** — secure login and account management
- **WhatsApp** — optional communication between users and providers
- **Google Play Services** — for Android app delivery

---

## 6. Push Notifications

ZinteBar sends push notifications to keep you informed about your order status (confirmed, rejected, delivered, etc.). Your device's FCM token is stored securely in our database solely for this purpose. You can disable notifications at any time from your device settings (Settings → Apps → ZinteBar → Notifications).

---

## 7. Data Retention

- Your account data is retained as long as your account is active
- Order history is retained to provide you with booking records
- You may request deletion of your account and data by contacting us

---

## 8. Data Security

We use Firebase's industry-standard security infrastructure to protect your data. Passwords are managed entirely by Firebase Authentication and are never stored or accessible by us. We apply Firebase Security Rules to restrict data access appropriately.

---

## 9. Children's Privacy

ZinteBar is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

## 10. User Rights

You have the right to:
- Access the personal information we hold about you
- Request correction of inaccurate information
- Request deletion of your account and associated data
- Withdraw consent for notifications at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.
